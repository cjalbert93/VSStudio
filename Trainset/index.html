Why?

"As a visual learner, I struggled through more than two decades of formal education in the sciences. During this process, I discovered the incredibly powerful capacity for the brain to understand concepts represented by moving objects.

Ultimately, I discovered that the world lacks a higher-learning Toolkit, so I got busy building one.

I am proud to welcome you early explorers to my humble endeavor. I hope you leave this place better than you came."

-Colin.

-Founder and CEO

What?

XTOWER is a Library of Dynamic Tools and Information built using a 3D Leveling System to organize the layers of Information. It covers a Massive portion of Higher Education Topics, specifically those in the Sciences.

The Level 0 Introductions are for Everyone and build the Foundation for the Learning Process. We Highly Recommend starting there.

If you are looking for any Specific Topic, you can use the Search Bar or Navigation Tab at the Top of the Page.

XTOWER is Free but Requires an Account if you want to keep Track of your Courses, Toolkits, Awards and Leveling.
