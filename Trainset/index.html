Why?

"As a visual learner, I struggled through more than two decades of formal education in the sciences. Throughout this process I discovered the incredibly powerful capacity for the brain to understand concepts represented by moving objects.

Ultimately, I discovered that the world lacks a dynamic library, so I got busy building one.

I am proud to welcome you early explorers to my humble endeavor. I hope you leave this place better than you came."

-Colin.

-Founder and CEO

What?

XTOWER is a dynamic library of visual tools and information built on a game-like leveling system. It is equip with a variety of amazing learning tools like our Reality Matrix and our Visual Knowledge Tree.

XTOWER was built as a 3-dimensional tower which uses a grid and leveling system to organize layers of tools and information. The layers follow approximately 5 levels for 1 academic year within the United States Educational System. For example, levels 1-5 is what you would learn in first grade, levels 6-10 what you would learn in second grade and so on.

The Lv. 0 Introductions are for everyone and build the foundation for the entire learning process. Start with those.

Below this tab are your Masteries and Trophies for completing the various layers and their challenges.

If you are looking for any specific topic, you can use the search bar below to find which levels those lessons are on. Otherwise, we strongly urge you to start from the bottom and take your time to enjoy the wonder that is learning.
