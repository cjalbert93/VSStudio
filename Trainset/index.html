Why?

"As a visual learner, I struggled through more than two decades of formal education in the sciences. During this process, I discovered the incredibly powerful capacity for the brain to understand concepts represented by moving objects.

Ultimately, I discovered that the world lacks a dynamic library, so I got busy building one.

I am proud to welcome you early explorers to my humble endeavor. I hope you leave this place better than you came."

-Colin.

-Founder and CEO

What?

XTOWER is a library of dynamic tools and information built on a game-like leveling system. It is equip with a variety of amazing learning tools like our Lv. 0 Reality Matrix. XTOWER is free but requires an account if you want to keep track of your courses, awards and leveling.

XTOWER was built as a 3-dimensional tower which uses a grid and leveling system to organize layers of tools and information.

The Lv. 0 Introductions are for Everyone and build the foundation for the entire learning process. No matter where you are in your education, we highly recommend taking all Lv. 0 courses before anything else.

Below are your Masteries and Trophies for completing the various layers and their challenges.

If you are looking for any specific topic, you can use the search bar above to go directly to those topic levels. Otherwise, we strongly urge you to start from the bottom and take your time to enjoy the adventure that is learning.
