Lv. 00: Welcome to Reality

In the beginning, time and space as we know it, did not exist.

But then, in an instant, it did.

An explosion of cosmic proportions for a fraction of second moving away from each other at impossible speeds.The forces of raw elementary energy forming the behaviors which would determine the laws of reality for the rest of time as we know it.

In this level we explore the incredible journey which we are on together. Strap in.

Lv. 00: Welcome to Humanity

Since the beginning of human civilization, words, writing, artwork and now incredible digital media has empowered humans to a seemingly impossible way of life.

Through storms and darkness, the human has persevered and become the caretakers of this incredible planet.

In this level, we explore the systems which make up our minds and discover the tools to unlock our reality.
